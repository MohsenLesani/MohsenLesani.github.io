 Bio Sketch


I am an associate professor at the Computer Science and Engineering Department of the University of California, Riverside. Before joining UCR, I was a postdoc at MIT and obtained my PhD from UCLA. My research interests are reliability and security of software systems especially concurrent and distributed systems. I received the NSF CAREER award in 2020. My research has been recognized as SIGPLAN Research Highlight in 2019, received the distinguished paper award at OOPSLA 2018 and has been supported by multiple NSF grants.
