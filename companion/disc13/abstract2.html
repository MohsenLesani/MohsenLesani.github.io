Impossibility of Local Progress and Opacity

Mohsen Lesani, Jens Palsberg
  We prove that for direct-update TM algorithms, opacity is incompatible with the local progress liveness criterion, even for fault-free systems. Our result implies that if TM algorithm designers want both opacity and local progress, they should avoid direct-update algorithms.
